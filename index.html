VISUAL VIEWER

This viewer uses your device camera to enlarge and enhance the live view in front of you.

Modes such as invert, monochrome and high contrast may make objects, text or signs easier to see.

IMPORTANT

This viewer is intended as a visual aid only.

It is not designed or certified to function as a mobility aid and must not be relied upon for navigation, crossing roads, avoiding hazards or any safety-critical activity.

Always use appropriate mobility aids, assistance or guidance when required.
