VISUAL VIEWER

This viewer uses your device camera to enlarge and enhance the live view in front of you.

Modes such as invert, monochrome and high contrast may help make text, objects or signs easier to see.


IMPORTANT

This viewer is intended only as a visual enhancement aid.

It is not designed or certified as a mobility aid and must not be relied upon for navigation, crossing roads, avoiding hazards, or any safety-critical activity.

Always use appropriate mobility aids or assistance where required.
